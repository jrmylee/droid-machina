Minesweeper:  Rules and Basics

The Objective: To empty the board of everything but mines.  The faster you clear the board, the better you score.

How to Play:

  • Uncover a mine, and you lose.
  • Uncover a blank, and you keep playing.
  • Uncover a number, and the number reveals how many mines are adjacent to that square.

To Start a Game:

Go to File->New Game.

To End a Game:

Go to File->Exit.
